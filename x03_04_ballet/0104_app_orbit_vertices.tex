 $$\cos \alpha={\frac {a^2 b \, \sqrt {-{a}^{2}-{b}^{2}+2\,\sqrt {{a}^{4}-{b}^{2}{c}^{2}}}}{{c}^{2}\sqrt {{a}^{4}-{c}^{2} x_1^{2}}}}.$$
